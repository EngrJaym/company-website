Get to Know Us!

Our Company

SmartPolls is an innovative online voting system developed by JAYM Solutions Inc., a leading software development company based in Rizal, Philippines. Founded in 2016, JAYM Solutions has grown into a trusted name in the software industry, serving clients across various sectors, including government, education, and private organizations.

At JAYM Solutions, we specialize in creating cutting-edge software solutions that streamline processes and enhance efficiency. With a focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, we strive to provide top-notch solutions that meet the needs of our clients. We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, integrity, and continuous improvement, ensuring that every project we undertake delivers exceptional results.

Our Vision & Mission

At SmartPolls, our vision is to revolutionize the way voting is conducted by leveraging technology to create a secure, reliable, and user-friendly platform for online voting. We envision a future where every eligible individual can participate in the democratic process conveniently and securely from anywhere in the world.

Our mission is to empower organizations, governments, and communities to conduct fair, transparent, and inclusive elections, referendums, surveys, and polls. We are committed to providing accessible tools and resources that enable our users to engage in democratic processes effectively, thereby fostering trust, accountability, and civic participation.

With a focus on innovation, integrity, and social responsibility, we strive to advance the principles of democracy and strengthen the foundations of governance worldwide. Our mission-driven approach guides everything we do, from product development to customer support, as we work towards creating a more democratic and equitable society.
